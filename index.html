Getting Started

The Town of Enfield Interactive Map provides public access to property records, zoning, school districts, flood zones, and more for all parcels within Enfield, CT.
Quick start: Use the search bar at the top right to find a property by address, Parcel ID, or Map and Lot number. Click a result to zoom to the parcel and load its details in the left panel.
💡 New to the app? A guided tour launches automatically on your first visit. You can relaunch it anytime from the help button.

Select Tool

Select
Click or draw on the map to select one or more parcels. Selected parcels highlight and their details load in the left panel.
  • 1
    Click the Select icon in the toolbar.
  • 2
    Use the dropdown to switch between point, rectangle, or polygon selection modes.
  • 3
    Click or draw on the map to select parcels.
  • 4
    Click Clear All to deselect.
💡 The Select tool defaults to the Parcels layer. Other selectable layers can be toggled in the panel.

Share

Share
Generates a shareable link to the current map view preserving zoom, location, and selected parcel.
  • 1
    Click the Share icon in the toolbar.
  • 2
    A shortened URL is generated automatically — copy it to your clipboard.

  • 4

Abutters Mail Label Generator

Mail Labels
A custom tool developed by the Town of Enfield GIS Division. Converts an abutters list into print-ready Avery 5160 mailing labels.
  • 1
    Use the Abutters tool to generate a list and export it as a ZIP file.
  • 2
    Open the Mail Label Generator from the toolbar.
  • 3
    Upload ZIP — upload the exported file.
  • 4
    Configure — set label options.
  • 5
    Generate — download the print-ready PDF.
💡 Intended for public hearing notifications requiring abutting property owner mailing lists.

Abutters (Near Me)

Abutters
Identifies parcels within a specified distance of a selected location. Commonly used for public hearing notification lists.
  • 1
    Click the Abutters icon in the toolbar.
  • 2
    Choose your location method: point, polyline, or polygon.
  • 3
    Set your buffer distance in Feet (default 30 ft).
  • 4
    Adjacent parcels are highlighted and listed with owner information.
  • 5
    Use the export icon to download a ZIP for the Mail Label Generator.

Measure

Measure
Calculates distances and areas on the map in real time. Supports distance and area modes with selectable units.
  • 1
    Click the Measure icon in the toolbar.
  • 2
    Select distance or area mode.
  • 3
    Choose units from the dropdown (Feet, Imperial, Meters, etc.).
  • 4
    Click on the map to place your first point, then continue clicking to add segments.
  • 5
    Double-click to finish. Use the trash icon to clear.

Print

Print
Exports the current map view as a printable PDF. Position and zoom the map first, then generate the output.
  • 1
    Zoom and pan the map to the area you want to print.
  • 2
    Click the Print icon in the toolbar.
  • 3
    Select a Template (e.g., A3 Landscape).
  • 4
    Check Show print area to preview the output boundary.
  • 5
    Click Print to generate and download the PDF.
⚠️ If nothing happens, your browser may be blocking pop-ups. Allow pop-ups from this site and try again.

FAQ & Troubleshooting

How do I find a specific property? ▼
Use the search bar at the top right. Search by street address, Parcel ID, or Map and Lot. If no results appear, try a shorter term — type "Hazard" instead of "100 Hazard Avenue."
Who owns this parcel? ▼
Select the parcel and open the Ownership section in the left panel.
Is this property in a flood zone? ▼
Select the parcel and scroll to Flood Zone & FEMA Information. Links to view and download FEMA FIRM panels are provided.
What zoning district is this parcel in? ▼
Select the parcel and open the Zoning section. For full regulations contact the Town of Enfield Planning & Zoning department.
Can I use this data for a legal survey or boundary dispute? ▼
No. Data is for informational and planning purposes only. Parcel boundaries are approximate. Consult a licensed land surveyor for official determinations.
How accurate is the parcel data? ▼
Data is provided by the Town of Enfield Assessor's Office and updated periodically. Verify critical information directly with the Assessor's Office.
Why isn't the map loading? ▼
Refresh the page. The map works best in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge. Clear your browser cache if issues persist.
Why don't certain layers show at some zoom levels? ▼
Some layers are scale-dependent. Try zooming in or out. The Legend indicates which layers are currently visible.
Nothing happens when I click Print — what do I do? ▼
Your browser may be blocking pop-ups. Allow pop-ups from this site and try again.
How do I zoom back out to see all of Enfield? ▼
Click the Home button (house icon) on the left side of the map.
The information for my property is incorrect — who do I contact? ▼
Contact the GIS Division or Assessor's Office using the information below.
How do I turn on aerial/satellite imagery? ▼
Click the Local Aerial Gallery icon in the toolbar. Select 2023 Spring, Color, 3 inch for the highest resolution.
